The Strongest Cowboy

PS. "The medium is the message" Marshal McLuhan

In the Wild West, there was a cowboy who was called the strongest there is.

This Cowboy was called the strongest because of his sharpshooting, and lasso skills. It was said that he could draw his pistol at the speed of sound, and whip his lasso even faster.

While travelling the country, he noticed the difference in wealth between the rich and the poor. He decided to do anything he could to fix it, starting with robbing a train with the richest people in the country.

He had succesfully managed to rob this train, but he got some very powerful people mad. Those powerful people retaliated by sending dirty cops to kill him.

The Cowboy had realized that the only way to ever find peace from these cops would be to stop being a cowboy. He had hung up his hat but kept his revolver in case the police ever found him with his new identity

A few years after the man retired, he entered a saloon, he immediately noticed something was wrong, it was suspicious. Looking around he only saw 5 people sitting around, drinking. This was prime time, the saloon should have been overflowing with customers, but it was empty.

3 of the 5 men drew revolvers, the other two decided to find cover, before drawing their revolvers. The cowboy disarmed all 5 of the men, by using a lasso to grab their guns from them.

The cowboy decided to escape and run away as he abandoned this life long ago. As he was running for the door, he saw the small militia of police officers gathered outside. It all depended on this moment; would he surrender? Or fight against at least 50 men

If you were the strongest would you fight or surrender?
