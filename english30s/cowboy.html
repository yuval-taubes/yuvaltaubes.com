The Strongest Cowboy

In the Wild West there was a cowboy who was called the strongest there is.

This Cowboy was called the strongest because of his sharpshooting. It was said that he could he could draw his pistol at the speed of sound.

While travelling the country, he noticed the difference in wealth between the rich and poor. He decided to do anything he could to fix it, starting with robbing a train with the richest people in the country.

He had succesfully managed to rob this train, but he got some very powerful people mad. Those powerfful people retaliated by sending dirty cops to kill him.

The Cowboy had realized that the only way to ever find peace from these cops would be to stop being a cowboy. He had hung up his hat, but kept his revolver in case the police ever found him with his new identity
